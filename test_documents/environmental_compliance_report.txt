KERALA STATE POLLUTION CONTROL BOARD
GOVERNMENT OF KERALA

CIRCULAR NO. KSPCB/ENV/2025/11

Date: September 8, 2025
Subject: Annual Environmental Compliance Report Submission

TO: All Industrial and Transportation Projects
FROM: Chairman, Kerala State Pollution Control Board

SUBMISSION DEADLINE: November 1, 2025
PRIORITY LEVEL: MEDIUM

All major transportation infrastructure projects, including metro rail systems, must submit their Annual Environmental Compliance Report for the year 2024-25.

REQUIRED SUBMISSIONS:

1. AIR QUALITY MONITORING
   - Monthly air quality data from all monitoring stations
   - Particulate matter (PM2.5, PM10) measurements
   - Noise pollution levels during construction and operation
   - Mitigation measures implemented

2. WATER MANAGEMENT
   - Rainwater harvesting implementation report
   - Wastewater treatment and recycling data
   - Ground water impact assessment
   - Water conservation measures

3. WASTE MANAGEMENT
   - Construction waste disposal records
   - Electronic waste management protocols
   - Recyclable material recovery statistics
   - Hazardous waste handling procedures

4. BIODIVERSITY CONSERVATION
   - Tree plantation and conservation efforts
   - Wildlife corridor maintenance
   - Green belt development progress
   - Ecological impact mitigation measures

5. ENERGY EFFICIENCY
   - Renewable energy integration report
   - Energy consumption optimization measures
   - Carbon footprint reduction initiatives
   - Solar power adoption statistics

SUBMISSION FORMAT:
- Digital submission through KSPCB portal
- Hard copy with authorized signatures
- Supporting documents and photographs
- Third-party audit reports

PENALTIES FOR NON-COMPLIANCE:
- Late submission: ₹50,000 fine
- Non-submission: ₹2,00,000 fine and show cause notice
- False reporting: Legal action under Environment Protection Act

This report must be prepared by the Environmental Department in coordination with the Engineering and Operations teams.

For technical assistance: env.support@kspcb.kerala.gov.in
For submission queries: compliance@kspcb.kerala.gov.in
