FIRE SAFETY AUDIT NOTICE
Ref No: KFRS/AUDIT/2025/METRO/12

Date: September 1, 2025
Subject: Mandatory Fire Safety System Audit and Certification

TO: Kochi Metro Rail Limited
FROM: Director General, Kerala Fire and Rescue Services

AUDIT SCHEDULE: December 15, 2025
RISK ASSESSMENT: LOW
DEPARTMENT: Safety

As per the Kerala Fire and Safety Act, 2021, all public transportation facilities must undergo mandatory fire safety audits every two years.

AUDIT SCOPE:

1. FIRE DETECTION SYSTEMS
   - Smoke detection sensors in all coaches
   - Fire alarm systems at stations
   - Automatic sprinkler system inspection
   - Emergency lighting functionality

2. EVACUATION PROCEDURES
   - Emergency exit accessibility
   - Evacuation route signage
   - Public address system for emergencies
   - Staff training on fire emergency protocols

3. FIRE FIGHTING EQUIPMENT
   - Fire extinguisher placement and maintenance
   - Hydrant system pressure testing
   - Emergency vehicle access routes
   - Fire department coordination protocols

4. ELECTRICAL SAFETY
   - Circuit breaker and fuse inspection
   - Cable insulation testing
   - Generator room fire safety
   - Transformer safety measures

5. DOCUMENTATION REVIEW
   - Fire safety management plan
   - Staff training records
   - Equipment maintenance logs
   - Previous audit compliance status

PRE-AUDIT REQUIREMENTS:
- Submit updated fire safety management plan
- Provide staff training completion certificates
- Equipment maintenance records for last 6 months
- Architectural drawings with fire safety annotations

AUDIT PROCESS:
- Physical inspection of all facilities
- Testing of fire safety equipment
- Review of documentation and procedures
- Staff interview and demonstration
- Compliance certificate issuance

COMPLIANCE DEADLINE: December 15, 2025

The audit will be conducted by certified fire safety engineers. Non-compliance may result in operational restrictions until deficiencies are addressed.

Contact for scheduling: audit.metro@keralafire.gov.in
Emergency coordination: emergency@keralafire.gov.in
