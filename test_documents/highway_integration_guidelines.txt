NATIONAL HIGHWAY AUTHORITY OF INDIA
MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS

CIRCULAR NO. NHAI/INTEGRATION/2025/08

Date: September 12, 2025
Subject: Metro-Highway Integration Compliance Guidelines

TO: All Metro Rail Operators
FROM: Chairman, National Highway Authority of India

IMPLEMENTATION DEADLINE: January 30, 2026
PRIORITY: MEDIUM
RESPONSIBLE DEPARTMENT: Engineering & Operations

New guidelines for metro rail integration with national highway infrastructure to ensure seamless multimodal transportation.

INTEGRATION REQUIREMENTS:

1. PHYSICAL CONNECTIVITY
   - Direct pedestrian access from highway bus stops to metro stations
   - Covered walkways and weather protection
   - Accessible ramps and elevators for differently-abled passengers
   - Clear directional signage in local and English languages

2. DIGITAL INTEGRATION
   - Common ticketing system with highway bus services
   - Real-time arrival information sharing
   - Mobile app integration for journey planning
   - Digital payment gateway synchronization

3. TRAFFIC MANAGEMENT
   - Coordinated traffic signal systems
   - Dedicated lanes for metro feeder buses
   - Parking facilities for private vehicles
   - Two-wheeler parking with security

4. SAFETY PROTOCOLS
   - CCTV coverage at integration points
   - Emergency response coordination
   - Security personnel deployment
   - First aid stations at major integration hubs

5. ENVIRONMENTAL STANDARDS
   - Noise pollution control measures
   - Air quality monitoring at integration points
   - Waste management systems
   - Green building certification for new structures

SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS:
- Integration feasibility study
- Traffic impact assessment
- Environmental clearance certificates
- Public consultation report
- Financial viability analysis

FUNDING SUPPORT:
Government will provide 60% funding for approved integration projects under the National Infrastructure Pipeline.

This initiative requires coordination between Engineering, Operations, and Administration departments.

Technical support: integration.support@nhai.gov.in
Project approval: projects@nhai.gov.in
