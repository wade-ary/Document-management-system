COMMISSIONER OF METRO RAIL SAFETY
GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
MINISTRY OF RAILWAYS

SAFETY DIRECTIVE NO. CMRS/25/2025

Date: September 10, 2025
Subject: Updated Track Inspection Guidelines and Safety Protocols

TO: All Metro Rail Operators
FROM: Commissioner of Metro Rail Safety

URGENT - COMPLIANCE REQUIRED BY: OCTOBER 15, 2025

This directive mandates immediate implementation of updated track inspection procedures for all metro rail systems under CMRS jurisdiction.

KEY REQUIREMENTS:

1. MONTHLY INSPECTIONS
   - Visual inspection of all track segments
   - Documentation of any defects or anomalies
   - Immediate reporting of critical issues

2. QUARTERLY ASSESSMENTS
   - Ultrasonic testing of rail joints
   - Geometric measurements of track alignment
   - Comprehensive safety audits

3. SAFETY PROTOCOLS
   - Enhanced worker safety measures
   - Updated emergency response procedures
   - Mandatory safety training for all personnel

COMPLIANCE DEADLINE: October 15, 2025

Failure to comply with these directives may result in operational restrictions and penalties.

All metro operators must submit compliance reports by the specified deadline.

This is a HIGH PRIORITY directive requiring immediate attention from the Engineering Department.
