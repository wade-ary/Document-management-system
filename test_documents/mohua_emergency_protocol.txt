MINISTRY OF HOUSING & URBAN AFFAIRS
GOVERNMENT OF INDIA

REGULATION UPDATE NO. MoHUA/2025/Emergency/09

Date: September 5, 2025
Subject: Emergency Response Protocol for Metro Operations

TO: All Urban Metro Rail Operators
FROM: Secretary, Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs

EFFECTIVE DATE: September 30, 2025
RISK LEVEL: HIGH

New emergency response protocols for metro operations during adverse weather conditions and emergency situations.

MANDATORY IMPLEMENTATION REQUIREMENTS:

1. WEATHER EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS
   - Enhanced communication systems during storms
   - Passenger evacuation procedures for flooding
   - Service suspension criteria for high winds
   - Coordination with local disaster management authorities

2. COMMUNICATION SYSTEMS
   - Real-time passenger information systems
   - Emergency broadcast capabilities
   - Direct hotline to emergency services
   - Mobile app emergency notifications

3. PASSENGER SAFETY MEASURES
   - Emergency evacuation drills (monthly)
   - Staff training on emergency procedures
   - Updated first aid facilities at all stations
   - Wheelchair accessible emergency exits

4. COORDINATION REQUIREMENTS
   - Liaison with city police departments
   - Fire and rescue services integration
   - Hospital emergency contact protocols
   - Media communication guidelines

COMPLIANCE DEADLINE: September 30, 2025

This regulation supersedes all previous emergency response guidelines. All metro operators must update their Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and submit compliance certificates.

Operations Department must lead implementation with support from Safety and Administration departments.
